Common Questions

FAQ

How much do your services cost? ↓
All services are custom-scoped and priced based on the complexity, size, and duration of each engagement. We provide transparent, itemized quotes after a free discovery call. There are no hidden fees.
How long does a typical engagement take? ↓
Timelines vary by service. A focused security audit may take 1–2 weeks, while a broader risk management program could span several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the scoping phase.
Do you work with small businesses? ↓
Absolutely. SSIK works with organizations of all sizes — from startups and small businesses to mid-size enterprises. We tailor our approach to match your resources and risk profile.
How do you handle confidential information? ↓
All client information is treated with strict confidentiality. We operate under NDA for every engagement, and all sensitive data is handled in accordance with applicable privacy regulations including PIPEDA.
Do you offer remote engagements? ↓
Yes. Most of our consulting work can be conducted fully remotely using secure communication and documentation tools. On-site visits can be arranged where required.
