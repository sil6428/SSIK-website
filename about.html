Who We Are

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Purpose

SSIK IT Consulting & Solutions — Secure Solutions in Information & Knowledge — was built on a straightforward conviction: organizations of every size deserve access to serious cybersecurity expertise delivered with professionalism and integrity.

We bring a combined 8+ years of experience in cybersecurity, IT auditing, and information systems — and an approach rooted in continuous learning, hands-on practice, and genuine commitment to our clients' security posture.

Our advisory model is pragmatic: we don't just identify problems and leave — we work alongside you to build lasting, sustainable security programs tailored to your organization's realities.
